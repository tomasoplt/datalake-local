The city's sporting tradition is closely linked to hockey. The HC Rytíři Kladno hockey club, one of the oldest in the Czech Republic, has produced a number of national team players and won the Czech Republic's championship title six times. Home games are played at the Evžen Pláteník Winter Stadium, which can accommodate 8,500 spectators.
Kladno's transport accessibility has significantly improved after the opening of the new D7+ motorway branch in 2019 and the planned D metro line, the extension of which to Kladno is scheduled for completion in 2031. The city also has the internationally certified KladnoTech science and technology park, which is home to over 140 companies focused on biotechnology, robotics and renewable energy sources.
